FAQ

Common questions, honest answers.

StudyMate is a free academic study manager for university students. Add your current semester subjects, organize learning items, manage your task list with deadlines, and view your timetable — all in one place.

Anything with a deadline — homework, assignments, quizzes, projects, presentations, mid-terms, and finals. Each task is linked to a subject so everything stays organized by course.

Once you add a task with a deadline, StudyMate automatically sends an email reminder 7 days before, then a follow-up 4 days before. No configuration needed — it just works.

No catch. StudyMate is completely free — no credit card, no premium tier, no locked features. Every tool is available to every user from day one.

Yes! The timetable view automatically highlights today's schedule so you can see your classes at a glance without counting which day of the week it is.

Registering takes under a minute. Adding your subjects and first tasks takes about 10 minutes. After that, just check in daily and StudyMate keeps you on track.
